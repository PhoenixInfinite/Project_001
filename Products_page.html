The Junkies Heven

TOOP

Marijuana, also known as ganja, is a psychoactive drug derived from the Cannabis plant, commonly used for its relaxing effects, medicinal benefits, and recreational purposes, with varying legal status worldwide.

HEROIN

Heroin, an opioid derived from morphine, is a highly addictive substance that produces intense euphoria and pain relief, often leading to severe health consequences and high potential for overdose.

Morphine

Morphine, a powerful opioid analgesic, is widely used in medical settings for severe pain management, known for its efficacy but also for the risk of dependence and addiction.

LSD

LSD (lysergic acid diethylamide) is a potent hallucinogenic drug that alters perception, mood, and thought, often leading to profound psychological experiences, with minimal risk of physical addiction.
